THE ORPHANAGE

VOLUNTEER PROGRAMME by our church allows us to work with the underprivileged children who have been isolated and many times forgoten by society
Depending on the needs of the children , volunteers can get involved in it and help out us much as they can
Volunteering in an orphanage is a great way to really experience the culture of communities whilst making a contribution and gaining an unforgettable experience

THE MERCY MINISTRY

AT THE MOST BASIC LEVEL mercy ministry is helping people with a practical need in the name of Jesus Christ ,this can take many forms and can be achieved in different ways
But why do we engage in mercy ministry? For many of us , we have experienced the challanges of poverty or the physical pain of an ailment that interrupted our lives and so we want others to experiencejoy too

Jesus calls us to care for those in need and to give in His name because when we do these things we are doing them unto Him
Matt. 25:34-40
